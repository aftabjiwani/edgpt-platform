6. Service Credits and Remedies

6.1 Service Credit Calculation

Service Credits are calculated as follows:

  • Uptime below 99.9%: 10% of monthly service fee
  • Uptime below 99.5%: 25% of monthly service fee
  • Uptime below 99.0%: 50% of monthly service fee
  • Uptime below 95.0%: 100% of monthly service fee

6.2 Credit Request Process

  1. Submit credit request within 30 days of incident
  2. Provide detailed incident description and impact
  3. GPT AI Corporation will investigate within 5 business days
  4. Credits applied to next billing cycle if approved
